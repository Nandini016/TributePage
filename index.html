KIM NAMJOON

Kim Nam-joon, known professionally as RM, is a South Korean rapper, songwriter, and record producer. He is the leader of South Korean boy band BTS. RM released his first solo mixtape, RM, in 2015, followed by his second mixtape, Mono, in 2018.

BTS leader Kim Namjoon is the latest victim of invasion of privacy. What has shocked fans of the Bangtan Boys is that the alleged culprit is a railway employee.

Here's the time line of Kim Namjoon

  • On December 20. 2013- RM and Jungkook appeared in the Perfect Christmas Music Video
  • January 7, 2013 - RM released his first pre-debut vlog as Rap Monster, sharing some of his thoughts for the upcoming year.
  • February 26, 2015 - RM was one of the original six cast members of South Korean variety show, Problematic Men. He appeared in the first 21 episodes but had to leave due to BTS’s The Red Bullet Tour.
  • On December 12, 2015 -RM appears on the show Same Bed, Different Dreams
  • May 30, 2019 - RM wins Best Male Solo Artist and six other group awards with BTS at the 14th Annual Soompi Awards.
  • January 16, 2020 - RM earned his first No. 1 on Billboard’s World Digital Song Sales chart after his collaboration with Younha, “Winter Flower (Feat. RM)” debuted at the top of the chart
  • September 12, 2020 - RM donates 100 million to the National Museum of Modern and Contemporary Art (MMCA) for art publications. The books will be donated to 400 public libraries throughout the country.
  • December 2, 2020 - RM debuted at No. 3 on Billboard’s Hot 100 Songwriters chart, making his first-ever appearance on the chart.
  • December 21, 2020 - RM has been recognized by the Arts Council Korea for his work as an art patron and support of the local arts.
  • On June 30, 2022 - Blooloop revealed that BTS’s RM has been nominated for the Blooloop 50 Museum Influencer list 2022, which recognizes individuals that have made an impact on the global museum sector
  • September 15, 2022 - RM makes a donation for the second consecutive year for the restoration and preservation of Korean cultural heritage overseas.
  • September 22, 2022 - RM is included on the Apollo 40 Under 40 Asia Pacific 2022 list for his influence on the art world
  • November 30, 2022 - BTS’s RM has been recognized for the impact he is making in the art world and has been selected as one of the 35 innovators in the 2022 Artnet Innovators List
  • December 3, 2022 - RM’s new solo album, Indigo, has topped the iTunes charts in at least 67 regions. Its title track, “Wild Flower (with youjeen)” topped the iTunes Top Songs chart in at least 87 regions
  • December 6, 2022 - RM took the top 3 spots on this week’s Billboard Hot Trending Songs chart with tracks from his new album, ‘Indigo.’
  • January 10, 2023 - RM’s ‘Indigo’ is ranked no. 71 on the Billboard 200 chart for the week ending on January 14. With this he becomes the first male South Korean soloist to spend 4 weeks on the chart.
  • March 30, 2023 - RM was announced as the brand ambassador for Bottega Veneta
  • January 2, 2023 - RM’s Indigo tops Billboard’s World Albums chart for a third week, becoming the longest-running no. 1 by a K-Pop soloist on the chart.
  • April 16, 2023 - RM’s second mixtape, ‘mono.’ becomes the third most-streamed album by a South Korean soloist in Spotify history
  • June 1, 2023 - RM becomes the third person ever to be appointed as ambassador for South Korea’s Ministry of National Defense’s Excavation Agency.
